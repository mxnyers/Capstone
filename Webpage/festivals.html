Manchester, Tennnessee

The annual four-day music festival developed and produced by Superfly Presents and AC Entertainment. It is held at Great Stage Park on a 650-acre (263 km2) farm in Manchester, TN. The festival begins on the second Thursday in June and lasts four days. Main attractions of this festival are the multiple stages featuring live music with a diverse array of musical styles including indie rock, classic rock, world music, hip hop, jazz, americana, bluegrass, country music, folk, gospel, reggae, pop, electronic, and other alternative music.
